ABOUT ME

Tuana Salihu

Hey there, let me give you a glimpse into the colorful mosaic that is me. Firstly, music is my heartbeat. Crafting beats and writing songs is where I find my flow, where my soul truly sings. There's an indescribable joy in piecing together melodies and 
weaving lyrics that resonate with emotion. And alongside my musical endeavors, I've got a knack for visual storytelling – logo design is my playground. There's a certain magic in bringing a brand's identity to life through creative visuals; it's like
painting a picture with pixels.


But it's not all rhythm and pixels; when I'm not lost in the world of music and design, I'm diving headfirst into the depths of history and the realms of art. Give me a dusty old tome on ancient civilizations or a book filled with the works of the
great masters, and I'm in my element. There's an endless well of inspiration to be found in the stories of the past and the brushstrokes of visionary artists.


Now, let's talk strings – the acoustic guitar is my trusted companion. Whether I'm strumming out a soulful ballad or picking through a lively melody, it's my go-to for unwinding and self-expression. 
And speaking of expression, I'm also delving into the world of computer science. HTML, CSS – they're not just letters and symbols; they're the building blocks of digital creativity. 
It's a whole new realm of possibility, and I'm eager to explore every corner of it.


In my younger days, I was all about the thrill of the game – football and basketball were my passions. But as time passed, I found a new rhythm on the tennis court. 
There's something about the grace and precision of tennis that speaks to me now.
And when it comes to art, there are two names that stir something deep within me – Vincent Van Gogh and Leonardo Da Vinci. 
Their masterpieces, their stories – they're a source of endless fascination and inspiration.
But perhaps the most exciting chapter in my story is the one I'm writing myself. I've got a book in the works – a tale of adventure, of love, of life's twists and turns. 
It's a journey of self-discovery, of weaving words into worlds, and I can't wait to share it with the world.

So, that's me in a nutshell – a symphony of passions, a tapestry of interests, and a journey of creative exploration.
